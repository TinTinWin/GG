Biography
	Rebecca Win was born at 15 February 1986 in Yangon who is a Burmese professional model and a popular advert actress on Burmese TV as well as a Burmese pop music singer.One of four siblings, Win was born May Hnin Nu to a Burmese father and a Kachin mother. She was moved to Yangon when she was just six months old. Win converted to Christianity while she joined the church choir at age six. As of 2006, she was a final year Japanese major at the University of Foreign Languages, Yangon.Win was first noticed when she entered Yangon-based City FM Radio's Music Idol contest in 2004.
